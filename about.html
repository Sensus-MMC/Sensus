About Sensus MMC

Sensus is a newsletter and news agency dedicated to the modded Minecraft community. We cover mod updates, modpack releases, technical changelogs, and notable happenings across the MMC scene.

What We Cover

Mod and modpack updates, community events, technical deep-dives, and editorial commentary on the state of modded Minecraft.

Contributing

Sensus is written by multiple contributors. Articles are attributed individually. If you have a tip or want to contribute, reach out through the community.
